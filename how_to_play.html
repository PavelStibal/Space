How to play Space

Controls

  • W - Move ship forward
  • A - Move ship left
  • D - Move ship right
  • S - Move ship back
  • Space - Shoot

Enemies

Enemies are aliens who shoot at you.
One of many enemies!

Powerups

But, not everything in space wants to kill you. Watch out for bonus items!
Shield: Adds a certain
amount of health		 Powerup: Upgrades weapon
